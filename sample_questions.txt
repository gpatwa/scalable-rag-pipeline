Sample Questions for RAG Pipeline Testing
==========================================

Use these questions to test the chat UI (http://localhost:8000) or the
API endpoint (POST /api/v1/chat/stream) against ingested content.


--- System Design Interview (from grok_system_design_interview.pdf) ---

Fundamentals & Concepts

1.  What is consistent hashing and why is it important?
2.  Explain the differences between SQL and NoSQL databases.
3.  What is database partitioning (sharding) and what are the common strategies?
4.  What are the trade-offs between availability and consistency in distributed systems?
5.  How does caching work in distributed systems and what caching strategies exist?

System Design Problems

6.  How would you design a URL shortening service like TinyURL?
7.  What are the key considerations for designing a video sharing platform like YouTube?
8.  How would you design a location-based service like Yelp?
9.  How would you handle video uploads at scale?
10. What are the requirements for designing a social network news feed?

Scalability & Infrastructure

11. How does load balancing work and what algorithms are used?
12. What is database denormalization and when should it be used?
13. How do you handle hot regions in a geographically sharded database?
14. What are the ACID properties and how do NoSQL databases handle them?
15. What is the CAP theorem and how does it affect system design?


--- Acme Corp (from sample documents) ---

Company

16. Who founded Acme Corp and when?
17. How much funding has Acme Corp raised and from whom?
18. Where are Acme Corp's offices located?

Product & Architecture

19. What is MedAssist and what technology stack does it use?
20. How does MedAssist's retrieval pipeline work?
21. What embedding model and LLM does MedAssist use?
22. What is the end-to-end latency of the MedAssist system?

Team & Process

23. Who is the VP of Engineering at Acme Corp and where did she come from?
24. What programming languages and frameworks does the Acme engineering team use?
25. What development methodology does the team follow?


--- Cloud Computing (from test_cloud.docx) ---

26. How do spot instances help reduce compute costs?
27. What is Karpenter and how does it relate to Kubernetes?
28. What are the cost optimization strategies for cloud workloads?
